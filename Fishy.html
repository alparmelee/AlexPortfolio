Fishy Business (WIP)

Role: Producer, UI Designer
Duration: September 2025 – December 2025 (WIP)
Team size: 7
Genre: Tower Defense, Mobile
Engine: Unreal Engine

Fishy Business is a 2D Tower Defense mobile game, set to release in December 2025. Catch fish in the water and sell them to grow your fisherman business and save your people! Currently I am the producer of the game and I also worked on UI design.

Project Management & Documentation

As the Producer, I lead over a team of seven, scheduling meetings and delegating tasks on a weekly basis. Again, communication is very important here. Team collaboration is crucial to ensure that all standards are set from the get-go and deadlines are met successfully. As Producer, I was also heavily involved in handling the documentation for the game, including the GDD, ASG, team contracts, and more.

User Interface (UI) Design

Main Menu Design

Designed for clear, immediate access to starting the game, quitting, shopping, and settings, with a visually appealing layout to engage users right away.

Pause Menu Design

A simple, focused interface that allows quick return to the game, restarting the level or quitting.

Reflection

Throughout development of Fishy Business, I’d say I’ve learned a good amount so far. I’ve learned that making a mobile game in Unreal Engine is harder than it looks, the importance of team communication and meetings, how rewarding the results can be when properly sticking to schedules and deadlines, and overall aspects of the multiple responsibilities a producer needs to juggle during production. In terms of UI Design, I’ve learned to adjust my PC minded brain to fit for mobile users, changing my workflow in the process.
