The Scale of Imagination

3rd Person Educational Platformer

The Scale of Imagination is a fun 3rd Person, educational, Foddyian platformer based on the scale of the universe simulation. The game takes the player on a journey through the size of existence, starting from the smallest theoretical units, to the entire size of the known universe. It implements a quiz-save system to educate the player as they make their way up the simulation. Our objects and theming as representative of childhood nostalgia and a dreamlike state.

I worked on this project with 6 other developers as a Producer and Lead Level Designer over the course of 2 months (Feb 2025 - Apr 2025).

My Accomplishments

  • Led a team of 7, organizing weekly tasks, roles, and project milestones for team members
  • Implemented anti-gravity mechanic and trampoline system using blueprints in Unreal Engine 5
  • Led development of game story, themes, and main level layout
  • Balanced a fun 3D platformer with educational elements that provide both information and enjoyment to the player
